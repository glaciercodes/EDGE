How to use the Article Generator

Welcome to GenZbot — your fast, friendly AI writing assistant. This tool turns simple keywords or short ideas into ready-to-use articles. Whether you're writing a blog, a newsletter, or a quick social post, GenZbot helps you create clear and useful content in seconds.

Start by typing the main topic or keyword. Next, add the style you want — for example: "friendly", "professional", or "funny". Choose a length (short, medium, or long) and press Generate Article. Our backend (hosted on Vercel) sends your request to the OpenAI model and returns a clean, readable article that you can copy, edit, or share instantly.

Why use GenZbot? Here are the main advantages:

  • Save time: Generate a first draft in seconds so you can focus on refining ideas, not getting started.
  • Consistent voice: Ask for a tone and GenZbot keeps style consistent across the whole piece.
  • Ideas on demand: Give a few keywords and receive a structured article with an intro, body points and conclusion.
  • Easy sharing: Copy or use the share button to send the article to teammates.
  • Multiple uses: Blog posts, product descriptions, email drafts, or study notes — all work well.

A few quick tips: give clear keywords, include a desired audience (e.g. "for beginners"), and pick a tone that matches your brand. If the article needs facts, double-check sources — the AI helps write, but you control accuracy.

We designed GenZbot to feel trustworthy and organized. The page highlights the main points, keeps language simple, and provides easy buttons to copy, share, or regenerate text. If you want more control, try adding constraints like word count or a required point list.

Finally, this tool is built to be simple: your keyword → choose tone and length → press generate. You're just a few clicks away from a usable article.
