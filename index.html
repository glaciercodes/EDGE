How to Use GenZbot

Welcome to GenZbot, your personal AI assistant designed to help you create high-quality content in seconds. Our tool is incredibly simple to use, yet powerful enough to handle all your content creation needs.

Getting Started

To begin using GenZbot, simply type your keywords or ideas into the input field. Be as specific or as general as you like - our AI understands context and can work with minimal information.

Pro Tip: The more details you provide about your desired article, the better the results will be. Include information about your target audience, tone preferences, and any specific points you want covered.

Customization Options

After entering your initial keywords, you'll have the option to customize your article further. You can specify:

  • Article length (from short summaries to comprehensive guides)
  • Tone (professional, casual, persuasive, etc.)
  • Target audience (experts, general readers, students, etc.)
  • Specific sections or points to include
